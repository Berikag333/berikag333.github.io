About Me

I specialize in translating analytics into action—combining rigorous analysis with clear storytelling so teams can prioritize effectively, optimize performance, and measure what truly matters.

  • Dashboards & decision-ready storytelling: Tableau, insight synthesis, stakeholder communication

Tools: Python (pandas), SQL, Tableau, Excel
